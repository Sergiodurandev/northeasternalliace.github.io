Developing quality affordable and sustaintable housing across Philadelphia

Impactful investing witha focus on sustainability and community improvement

Established in 2017, Northeastern Alliance LLC is a real estate investment firm dedicated to revitalizing Philadelphia's urban landscape. Our core mission is to acquire and meticulously restore neglected and dilapidated properties, thereby eliminating blight and contributing to the community's overall improvement.
 
Our diverse real estate portfolio encompasses properties throughout Philadelphia, with a strategic emphasis on the Germantown and North Philadelphia neighborhoods. Through our commitment to sustainable investment and community enhancement, we strive to create vibrant, thriving neighborhoods that benefit all residents.

Recent Projects

Our housing feature...

  • Our apartments boast modern, fully electric kitchens and bathrooms equipped with the latest appliances. We have updated sidewalks and renovated facades to enhance the aesthetic appeal and functionality of our properties. In our commitment to sustainability, we strive for 100% electric usage, having already converted the majority of our apartments from gas to electric for heating, stoves, and hot water.
  • We adhere to all current guidelines and requirements set by the City of Philadelphia, ensuring our properties have rental licenses, lead-safe certifications, and annual fire panel and fire extinguisher certifications. Additionally, we maintain certificates of rental suitability to provide a safe and compliant living environment for our tenants.

Community Impact

We have succesfully advocated for...

  • We have planted over 30 trees, providing summer shade and cooling the streets while enhancing the neighborhood's beauty.
  • Our efforts have also led to the cleanup of more than 20 vacant lots and the removal of numerous instances of graffiti and vandalism across the city.
  • We are proud to partner with Rapid Rehousing, Friends Rehabilitation, and Pathways to Housing to provide quality affordable housing to those most in need.
